INFO

Format

  • 10 rounds × 10 questions (100 total).
  • 30 seconds per question.
  • All trivia is from the Ricky Gervais Show and XFM radio show featuring Karl Pilkington.
  • Mostly typed answers, with one multiple-choice round.
  • Answers are case-insensitive and must match exactly.
  • Points are awarded for speed + accuracy.
  • Leaderboard is shown between rounds only.

Rules

  • One device per ticket (one ticket = one player).
  • No spelling variants — We’ve picked unambiguous answers.
  • Keep it fun. Don’t be a knobhead.
  • Host decisions are final (even if you’re fuming).

How you'll join

  • You’ll watch via a YouTube livestream link and answer in our quiz app.
  • We’ll send the livestream and app link 1 hour before kickoff.

Privacy

  • This is a fan-made quiz event.
  • We only collect the minimum information needed to process ticket purchases, manage entries, and contact participants about the event.
  • Payment is handled through our checkout provider. We do not store your full card details on this site.
  • We do not sell your personal data or share it for advertising.
  • Your email may be used to send your quiz access details, event updates, and important ticket information.
  • Your information will be deleted after the event upon request.
